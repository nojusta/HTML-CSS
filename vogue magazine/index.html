A Brief History

Of Jokūbas

  • 2005

    Commenced formal piano training, showcasing exceptional talent at a young age.

  • 2010

    Awarded first prize in the National Piano Competition, marking a significant milestone in his early career.

  • 2014

    Made his international debut, captivating audiences with a spellbinding recital at a renowned concert hall.

  • 2017

    Collaborated with esteemed orchestras, delivering captivating performances of iconic piano concertos.

  • 2019

    Released his debut solo album, receiving critical acclaim for his interpretation and musical sensitivity.

  • 2021

    Received the prestigious Rising Star Award, recognizing his exceptional achievements and promising future in the world of classical music.
